    Welcome Message

    From the President of OBU Student Union

    Dear Fellow Students,

    It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to the official platform of the Oda Bultum University Student Union. Our mission is to advocate for your rights, enhance your university experience, and foster a vibrant campus community.

    This academic year, we're focused on improving academic support services, addressing dormitory concerns, and expanding student welfare initiatives. We encourage every student to actively participate in union activities and share feedback to help us serve you better.

    - Nafri Ahmed
    Student Union President

    About OBU Student Union

    Our mission, vision, and values

    The Oda Bultum University Student Union is the official representative body for all undergraduate and postgraduate students. We work to ensure student interests are represented at all levels of university governance.

    Established in 2015 alongside the university, our union has grown to become a vital part of campus life, advocating for student rights, organizing events, and providing essential services.
